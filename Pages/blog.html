January 1st, 2021

12:00 AM

HAPPY NEW YEAR!!! SOSA is extremely excited to announce the launch of an all new overnight weekend camp for kids! Anyone from ages 13-18 is welcome to come join us at Fort Lauderdale to learn about water pollution, how it's affecting us and marine life, and about our drone missions. Kids will participate in hands-on team building activities that are sure to get the smiling in no time! Registration begins now and is open until January 15th or until spots run out. Hurry! There are only 25 spots due to social distancing regulations. Unfortunately our beach haulovers have been rescheduled for March 13th, 27th, and April 1st due to COVID-19. Here's to a new year!!
