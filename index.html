Save Our Sea Animals




Estimates suggest that 1.4 billion pounds of trash ends up in our oceans and 100 million marine animals are killed each year from plastic pollution. These are shocking numbers we hope to change.

Peanut's Story


As a baby turtle, Peanut got caught in a 6-pack ring. She couldn't get out, and as she grew, Peanut learned to live with the plastic around her waist. Due to the deformation, some of her organs failed to develop completely. Thankfully, she was found in Missouri and was taken to a zoo in St. Louis.
